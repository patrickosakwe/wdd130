Abuja

Abuja is the capital city of Nigeria, in the middle of the country. The skyline of the city, which was built largely in the 1980s, is dominated by Aso Rock, an enormous monolith. The Abuja City Gate is a concrete monument and national landmark. The gate is topped with the Nigerian flag and the coat of arms. It was erected in 1991, during the movement of the national capital from Lagos to Abuja.
