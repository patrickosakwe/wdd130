History

Our Story: Navigating the Heart of LagosEvery unforgettable journey begins with a single drop of water and a shared vision. For Rafting Travel, that vision came alive over two decades ago.The Spark (2002)Our story began in 2002. Driven by a deep passion for the water and a desire to showcase the vibrant, hidden natural beauty of Lagos, founders Patrick Osakwe and Joy Oguche set out on a mission. Armed with over 15 years of combined watercraft and guiding experience, they looked at the bustling coastlines and saw an opportunity to give people something rare: a true, unplugged escape right on the water.They realized that amidst the fast-paced energy of the city, people needed a place to connect, challenge themselves, and experience the thrill of the currents. With just a handful of quality rafts and an absolute commitment to safety, Rafting Travel was born.Pioneering the Lekki-Ajah Channels We started small, right in our own backyard. Our early days were defined by exploring and mastering the unique coastal waterways of the Lekki-Ajah region. Patrick and Joy personally guided those first trips, teaching locals and visitors alike how to read the tides, handle the paddles, and embrace the sheer joy of being out on the open water. Our reputation grew by word of mouth—passengers loved the unique blend of professional expertise, premium safety standards, and the warm, welcoming atmosphere that Patrick and Joy brought to every single excursion. Charting New Waters: Eko Atlantic as our community of adventurers grew, so did our desire to push boundaries. We expanded our horizons, taking our signature rafting experiences into the breathtaking, expansive Eko Atlantic waters. This expansion allowed us to offer a completely unique perspective of the city—where modern architectural marvels meet the raw, timeless energy of the ocean currents. Whether navigating the calm channels of Lekki or catching the coastal breeze near Eko Atlantic, our goal has always remained the same: providing an elite, thrilling, and incredibly safe adventure.
